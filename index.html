Purpose of This Survey

This survey aims to understand employees’ overall experience at Six-B Food Industries by gathering feedback on key areas such as job satisfaction, management, teamwork, communication, growth opportunities, compensation, work-life balance, and workplace culture. It is designed to capture how employees feel about their roles, responsibilities, and the support they receive from the organization. Identifies strengths and areas for improvement, helping management make better decisions and improve policies. Its goal is to boost employee engagement, satisfaction, and create a more positive and productive work environment.

All responses are completely confidential and anonymous. Please answer every question as honestly as possible.
